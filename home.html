Rules for Aqua Pong Baseball

This is not your traditional beer pong game!

1. Click Begin to start the game

2. Each game will be cut off at 10 seconds

3. Click "Throw" button to throw the pong ball

4. Time the click with the moving pong ball

5. 3 misses and the game is over

6. Score as many points as possible before time limit or 3 misses

7. Share and compete with your friends!
